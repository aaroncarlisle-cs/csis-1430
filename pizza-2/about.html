About Us

Hey bro,

Blake Chad here (founder and CEO of Pizza Bros) and I just wanted to give you a huge shoutout for stopping by. Here at Pizza Bros we serve up only the raddest of pizzas around!

Blake Chad

Founder and CEO of Pizza Bros

It all started in 1992 when I dropped out of high school to pursue my dream of becoming a professional skateboarder. One day while hopped up on Red Bull and trying to impress the ladies, I biffed an ollie and took a skateboard straight to the groin. When I woke up in the hospital, they told me I would never skate again. I spent the next three years in a dark, downward spiral. Worried that I was throwing my life away, my dad approached with an ultimatum:

You can either get your GED and go to college, or, I will give you $200 dollars to start a business.

Suffice it to say, I took the money!

With a $39 annual subscription to Geocities and the help of my nerdy kid brother, we launched Pizza Bros in the Summer of 1996. Since then, we have been serving Killer Pizzas for Killer Prices! Our commitment to using only the finest ingredients is why we have been voted both 3rd and 4th best pizza joint in all of Wyoming for 2003 and 2006! With locally sourced cheese grown on a nearby farm, it is easy to see why we have over nineteen 4-star reviews on Yelp.

So go ahead and give us a try,

you won't be dissapointed!

-Blake Chad
